    Snapper

    Screen capturing & recording for Android devices based on my experiment on Electron (Atom Shell)

    Snapper lets you take screenshots and record the screen of Android devices.

    e.g.

    Take screenshots

    Record screen

    Download

    • Snapper-v0.0.1-mac.zip
    • As of now, Windows and Linux prebuilts are not available yet but you can build them from source

    Requirements

    The target host (where the devices are connected) must still have ADB installed and either already running (e.g. via adb start-server) or available in $PATH.
